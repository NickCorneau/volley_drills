## 3. Field testing gate (D91, amended by D119)

Per `D119`, the D91 field-test gate now runs against v0b (not v0a). v0b adds the surfaces that carry the remaining validation risk: onboarding, home state, repeat path, and session summary with reason trace. Thresholds and enrichment requirements are unchanged from D91.

### Quantitative bar

- **5+** testers each complete **2+** sessions within **14 days**
- **Kill signal:** fewer than 3 of 5 start a second session within 14 days
- **Banded reading on the raw count:** `0-1/5` strong negative, `2/5` ambiguous, `3/5` weak pass of floor (`continue investigating`, not `go`), `4-5/5` first genuinely encouraging outcome (especially with unprompted self-initiation)
- **Interpretation aid (not an independent gate):** `>50%` review completion rate; separate self-initiated vs human-prompted second-session starts; per-session context log (location, surface, weather, time-of-day, blocker) for completed and missed sessions; post-qualification Ellis / Superhuman "how disappointed would you be?" check after a tester crosses the 2-in-14 line. See `docs/research/d91-retention-gate-evidence.md`.

### Pre-test checklist

1. Deploy v0b to Cloudflare Pages per `docs/ops/deploy-cloudflare-pages.md`
2. Test Add to Home Screen on the current public iOS line at test time (iOS 26.x as of April 2026; see `D57`). Confirm the installed HSWA loads, reports `display-mode: standalone`, and survives a phone restart with Dexie data intact. Do not gate field testing on `persisted() === true` — per `D118`, the Home Screen carveout alone is sufficient durability for a 14-day cohort, and `persist()` is heuristic; the observable state matrix is the thing to sanity-check, not a single boolean.
3. Verify review submit -> complete flow on the deployed v0b build (FB-11 folded into v0b acceptance)
4. Stand up the per-tester capture sheet specified in `docs/discovery/phase-0-wedge-validation.md` → *Decision gate* → *Capture per tester*. Out-of-band fields (return attribution self-initiated vs human-prompted, missed-session context log, post-qualification Ellis conviction check) are founder-tracked via check-ins, not app telemetry. In-app fields (session timestamps, end-early reasons, per-block completion, review submission) are already persisted on `ExecutionLog` + `SessionReview` and are exportable via V0B-15 once that lands; before V0B-15 ships, extract them manually from IndexedDB after each tester's 14-day window.
5. Hold founder-to-tester contact cadence constant across the window. Do not add extra nudges to drifting testers (contamination control per `D91`).
6. Run the cohort against the operational protocol in `docs/research/pre-telemetry-validation-protocol.md`: freeze the one-page preregistered decision memo before kickoff; use the verbatim recruitment and consent scripts; use three signal-based pulses at days 3 / 7 / 11 rather than daily check-ins; send the 72-hour forced-choice + open-text non-returner probe on any silent gap after a realistic opportunity; maintain the per-session ledger (with confidence flag and separate researcher-interpretation column) on the out-of-band fields above; begin the final readout with disconfirming evidence; and produce the 5-page evidence packet (preregistration, roster, ledger summary, evidence board with negative cases first, verdict memo). This protocol operationalizes `D91` without changing its thresholds.

### D104 scoping notes (why V0B-11..V0B-15 and V0B-28..V0B-29 exist)

`D104` (2026-04-16; refined same day) sets a 50-attempt minimum window combined with a Bayesian posterior rule (`P(p_corrected ≥ 0.70) ≥ 0.80`; `38/50` corrected, `41/50` raw pre-calibration proxy, `42/50` for injury-sensitive) and hysteresis on the downside on top of the existing `D80` 70% latent target. v0b does **not** need to ship the progression engine — that is M001-build scope — but v0b does ship the first user-facing surfaces that could claim or imply progression (`Session summary`, `review`, future `weekly receipt`), and it is cheap to seat two tiny pieces of the three-layer bias correction now.

The v0b items fall into three groups:

- **Data-shape and honesty rail (already scoped):** `V0B-11` no-signal floor at `N < 50`, `V0B-12` drill-variant grain, `V0B-13` `N` alongside any %, `V0B-14` set-window marker as a real setup step, `V0B-15` raw JSON export for `O12` replay.
- **First-layer bias correction (new):** `V0B-28` forced-criterion prompt + "If unsure, Not Good" on the review screen. Copy-only, zero behavior change, implements layer 1 of the three-layer correction.
- **Third-layer scaffolding (new, schema-only):** `V0B-29` reserves an optional `borderlineCount` on `SessionReview` so M001-build can wire the boundary-triggered 10-second borderline-review on top without a migration, and so any tester data that happens to carry the value replays cleanly against the corrected-Bayesian rule.

Full reasoning, posterior math, bias priors, and comparable-product analogues in `docs/research/binary-scoring-progression.md`.

What these items explicitly are not:

- They do **not** enforce the progression gate. A strong `goodPassRate` with `N >= 50` in v0b still only produces a user-facing "not yet, keep going" read; the gate — raw proxy today, corrected-Bayesian once calibration anchors exist — is wired in M001 build.
- They do **not** ship the second layer of the three-layer correction (the periodic 20-attempt athlete-plus-expert/video anchor block and the shrunken EWMA-updated `personalBiasOffset`).
